## Vision
Create the reference data infrastructure for territorial, economic and social intelligence in Africa, starting with Benin.

TEDI provides **structured, enriched, AI-ready datasets** across multiple sectors:
- Agriculture
- Real Estate
- Employment
- Business

## Problem Statement
- Reliable territorial data in Africa is fragmented, outdated or inaccessible.
- Institutions, banks, NGOs and AI companies lack trustworthy local datasets.
- Existing platforms expose raw data but do not provide **usable indices or signals**.

## Solution
A centralized data platform that:
- Collects legal raw datasets
- Normalizes and enriches them
- Produces indices, scores and APIs
- Enables B2B, institutional and AI usage

## Target Customers
- Banks & insurance companies
- Governments & public agencies
- NGOs & international organizations
- Startups & research labs
- AI companies (via licensing / partnerships)

## Value Proposition
- Clean & documented datasets
- Territorial indices ready for decision-making
- AI-ready labels and metadata
- Continuous updates

## Success Metrics
- Number of indexed zones
- Active API consumers
- Paid institutional contracts
- Dataset licensing agreements
